Ingredients
- 3 eggs
- Pinch of salt
- Pinch of pepper
- 1 slice cheese

Directions
1. In a small skillet, add 1 tbsp olive oil and heat over medium.
2. Crack eggs into skillet
3. Mix eggs with spatula in skillet
4 After about 4 minutes of mixing and flipping eggs continuouesly, add cheese.
5. Server after cheese has melted.
